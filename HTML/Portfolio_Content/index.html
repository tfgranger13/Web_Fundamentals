Bio

Timothy Granger grew up in a small town in Connecticut. He has always loved computers, from breaking apart a Commodore 64 to see how it worked when he was 8 to recently assembling his 3rd computer. He received his undergraduate degree from Northeastern University with a Bachelor of Science in Criminal Justice with a Minor in Mechanical Engineering. He received his graduate degree from Central Connecticut State University with a Master of Arts in Teaching.
